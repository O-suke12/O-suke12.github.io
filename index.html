About

Hi! I am currently a senior undergraduate student at Waseda Univ., majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. My research is focused on Safe Reinforcement Learning under uncertainty for robots autonomy, as well as MARL including human. I am a part of Intelligent Sofware Lab, advised by Prof. Toshiharu Sugawara and also advised by Prof. Ynao Tomohiro.

Interests

・Reinforcement learning
・Multi-agent system
・Spacecraft attitude/orbital control
・Human-AI interaction

Experience

I am currently working as a Machine Learning Engineer on an internship at Ghelia, where I work on scheduling optimization using reinforcement learning.
